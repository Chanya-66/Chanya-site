Hey! I'm Chanya — a Robotics & Machine Intelligence student at NUS, and honestly, my journey here was anything but a straight line. From ITE to Singapore Poly to NUS, every step taught me something new about myself and what I'm capable of.

By day I'm debugging robots and building intelligent systems. By night I'm probably deep into a K-drama or Japanese series, crying over a friends-to-lovers storyline. No regrets. 💅

I'm someone who laughs with you when you laugh, and cries with you when you cry — that's just how I show up for people. I love surprising the ones I care about in the best ways. 🤍

The two most important people in my world? My 6-year-old sister who I love to death, my mom — the one constant thing in my life and my dad, who I will always be grateful for. Everything I do, I do with them in mind.
